Visual Communication and Design (VCD)

I'm a student at Eastern Washington University studying Visual Communication Design. Initially I wanted to go into accounting, then business, and after trying a few classes in VCD I realized that's what I want to do. Art was a part of my life since I was a kid, I liked to draw and make sketches. Now studying VCD as my major and business for minor degree.
